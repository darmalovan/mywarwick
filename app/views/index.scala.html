My Warwick

My Warwick is a mobile app that brings together information about many aspects of your life at Warwick. You can use it to view your timetable, check your email, get bus times, see a weather forecast, and more.

My Warwick sends you notifications when something needs your attention. You'll get a notification when an assignment is due soon, a library book is due back soon, and so on. Your department can send you notifications if a lecture is moved or cancelled. So you'll always be in the loop.
